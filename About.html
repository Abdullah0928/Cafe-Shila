About Us

Welcome to Cafe shila! We're a 2000/02/10-established eatery dedicated to serving delicious, locally-inspired dishes in a friendly atmosphere. Our chef, Miss. Thakshila, is passionate about crafting mouthwatering meals using the freshest ingredients. At Cafe shila, we believe in creating a cozy space where good food and good times come together. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special celebration, our goal is to make every visit memorable. Come join us on a culinary adventure, where flavors meet warmth, and every bite tells a story. We can't wait to welcome you to Cafe shila for a delightful dining experience!
